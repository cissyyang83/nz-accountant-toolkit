# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want NZ Accountant Toolkit to be a respectful, practical, and safe place for accountants, bookkeepers, advisors, developers, and students to collaborate.

We welcome people with different levels of accounting, tax, software, and open-source experience.

## Expected Behaviour

- Be respectful and professional.
- Assume good intent.
- Explain corrections clearly.
- Welcome questions from new contributors.
- Keep discussion focused on the work.
- Avoid sharing confidential client details.

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Publishing confidential or identifying client information.
- Presenting personal tax advice as official advice from the project.
- Repeatedly disrupting discussions or pull requests.

## Reporting

If you see behaviour that concerns you, contact the repository maintainer through the relevant GitHub issue, discussion, or private contact channel if available.

Maintainers may remove content, close discussions, or restrict participation where needed to protect the project community.
