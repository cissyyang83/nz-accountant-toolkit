# GST Review Checklist

Use this checklist when preparing or reviewing GST returns and annual GST reconciliations.

## Client Details

- Client name recorded.
- GST number or IRD number recorded.
- GST basis confirmed.
- Filing frequency confirmed.
- Return period confirmed.

## Sales and Output Tax

- Sales agree to accounting records.
- Zero-rated sales reviewed.
- Exempt income reviewed.
- Asset disposals reviewed.
- Private use adjustment considered.

## Purchases and Input Tax

- Supplier invoices retained where required.
- Non-deductible items reviewed.
- Entertainment adjustment considered.
- Motor vehicle and private use reviewed.
- Large asset purchases reviewed.

## Reconciliation

- GST return agrees to GST report.
- GST control account reconciled.
- Inland Revenue filing history checked.
- Payments and refunds matched.
- Differences explained.

## Risk Review

- Land transactions reviewed for GST treatment.
- Imported services considered.
- Mixed-use assets considered.
- Bad debt adjustments reviewed.
- Change of GST basis or registration status considered.

## Sign-Off

- Prepared by.
- Reviewed by.
- Outstanding issues.
- Final filing status.
