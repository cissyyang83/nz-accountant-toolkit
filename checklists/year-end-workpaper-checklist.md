# Year-End Workpaper Checklist

Use this checklist when preparing annual accounts and income tax returns for New Zealand clients.

## General

- Client details confirmed.
- Balance date confirmed.
- Engagement scope confirmed.
- Prior year closing balances agreed.
- Trial balance reviewed.

## Bank and Cash

- Bank statements obtained.
- Bank reconciliations completed.
- Unreconciled items reviewed.
- Cash on hand confirmed where applicable.

## GST and Payroll

- GST reconciled.
- PAYE and payroll accounts reviewed.
- Inland Revenue balances checked.
- Payroll reports agreed to accounts.

## Income and Expenses

- Income completeness reviewed.
- Expense classifications reviewed.
- Entertainment add-back considered.
- Private use adjustments considered.
- Home office claims supported.

## Assets and Loans

- Fixed asset additions reviewed.
- Disposals recorded.
- Depreciation reviewed.
- Loan balances agreed to statements.
- Interest allocation reviewed.

## Companies

- Shareholder current accounts reconciled.
- Dividends supported by resolutions.
- Imputation credit account reviewed.
- Shareholder salaries reviewed.

## Trusts

- Trustee resolutions obtained.
- Beneficiary distributions reviewed.
- Beneficiary current accounts reconciled.
- Trust deed requirements considered.

## Finalisation

- Tax adjustments reviewed.
- Return labels checked.
- Client queries cleared.
- Senior review completed.
- Filing approval retained.
