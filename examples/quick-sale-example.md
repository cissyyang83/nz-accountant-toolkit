# Quick Sale Example

## Scenario

A client sells a residential property after a short ownership period. The practitioner needs to review whether the disposal may be taxable.

| Item | Amount |
| --- | ---: |
| Sale price | NZD 900,000 |
| Purchase price | NZD 760,000 |
| Legal and acquisition costs | NZD 7,500 |
| Improvement costs | NZD 48,000 |
| Agent and sale costs | NZD 22,000 |

## Draft Adjustment

```text
900,000 - 760,000 - 7,500 - 48,000 - 22,000 = 62,500
```

## Review Notes

- Confirm bright-line application.
- Review main home exclusion.
- Consider intention at acquisition.
- Check associated person rules.
- Review GST treatment if connected to taxable activity.
