# Trust Accounting Example

## Scenario

A family trust owns a rental property and has investment income. The accountant is preparing annual accounts and tax returns.

## Workpaper Areas

- Rental income and expenses.
- Beneficiary distributions.
- Beneficiary current accounts.
- Trustee resolutions.
- Capital expenses.
- Repairs and maintenance.
- Loan interest.
- GST status if any taxable activity exists.

## Review Notes

- Confirm trust deed requirements.
- Retain trustee minutes or resolutions.
- Check beneficiary allocations before filing.
- Separate income distributions from capital movements.
- Reconcile beneficiary current accounts.
