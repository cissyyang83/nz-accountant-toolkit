# Property Development Cost Tracker

## Purpose

The Property Development Cost Tracker helps accountants organise project costs, GST treatment, sale proceeds, and tax review notes.

## Use Cases

- Residential subdivision.
- Build-to-sell projects.
- Land held by developers or builders.
- Mixed capital and revenue land projects.
- Related-party development structures.

## Key Workpaper Sections

- Project summary.
- Land acquisition.
- Development costs.
- Holding costs.
- Funding and shareholder advances.
- GST tracking.
- Sale proceeds.
- Cost allocation by lot.
- Profit summary.
- Tax review notes.

## Review Questions

- Is the client carrying on a taxable property development activity?
- Was the land acquired with a purpose or intention of disposal?
- Is the client associated with a dealer, developer, or builder?
- Was GST zero-rating correctly applied?
- Are costs allocated on a reasonable basis?
- Are related-party transactions documented?
- Has work in progress been reviewed at balance date?
