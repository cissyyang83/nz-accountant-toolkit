# FIF Calculator

## Purpose

The FIF Calculator helps practitioners compare draft Foreign Investment Fund income under FDR and CV methods.

## Inputs

- Client name.
- Tax year.
- Investment name.
- Opening market value.
- Closing market value.
- Purchases.
- Sales proceeds.
- Dividends.
- Foreign tax deducted.
- Currency conversion method.

## Outputs

- FDR income estimate.
- CV income estimate.
- Difference between methods.
- Review warnings.
- Notes for tax return workpapers.

## Practitioner Notes

The calculator should not automatically decide the correct method. It should support review by showing the calculation outcomes and prompting the practitioner to confirm eligibility, exemptions, and method selection.

## Example Output

```text
FDR income: NZD 4,000
CV income: NZD 3,600
Suggested review: Confirm whether CV method is available and whether the investment is subject to any special rules.
```
