# Quick Sale Calculator

## Purpose

The Quick Sale Calculator helps prepare a structured workpaper for property disposal reviews.

## Inputs

- Property address.
- Acquisition date.
- Disposal date.
- Purchase price.
- Sale price.
- Improvement costs.
- Legal fees.
- Agent fees.
- GST status.
- Property use.
- Development or subdivision activity.
- Associated person details.

## Outputs

- Draft gain or loss.
- Taxability review prompts.
- GST review prompts.
- Evidence checklist.
- Reviewer conclusion field.

## Professional Judgement Required

The calculator can show a draft adjustment, but it cannot determine the tax outcome by itself. A practitioner should review the land rules, client intention, use of the property, timing, entity activity, and any exemptions.
