# Shareholder Current Account Tracker

## Purpose

The Shareholder Current Account Tracker helps monitor balances between a company and its shareholders.

## Inputs

- Opening balance.
- Drawings.
- Personal expenses paid by the company.
- Repayments.
- Dividends.
- Shareholder salaries.
- PAYE or withholding amounts.
- Imputation credits.
- Interest charged.

## Outputs

- Closing balance by shareholder.
- Overdrawn balance warning.
- Dividend and salary summary.
- Interest review prompt.
- Director resolution checklist.

## Review Notes

Overdrawn shareholder balances should be reviewed before finalising annual accounts. Practitioners should consider documentation, repayment plans, dividends, salaries, interest, and other tax consequences.
