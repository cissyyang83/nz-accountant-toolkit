# Xero Bank Reconciliation SOP

## Purpose

This SOP provides a standard process for reconciling bank accounts in Xero before GST filing, management reporting, or year-end accounts preparation.

## Scope

Use for New Zealand small business clients using Xero bank feeds.

## Procedure

1. Confirm the bank feed is active.
2. Compare the Xero bank balance to the actual bank statement.
3. Reconcile all bank statement lines.
4. Review unreconciled transactions.
5. Check duplicate bank feed lines.
6. Review suspense, clearing, and unknown transaction accounts.
7. Check GST coding on material transactions.
8. Review private or shareholder payments.
9. Export or save reconciliation reports.
10. Record preparer and reviewer sign-off.

## Review Areas

- Old unreconciled statement lines.
- Manual journals posted to bank accounts.
- Transfers between bank accounts.
- Personal spending coded to business accounts.
- GST claimed on non-deductible or private items.
- Loan repayments split between principal and interest.
- Merchant settlement timing differences.

## Evidence to Retain

- Bank statement.
- Xero reconciliation report.
- Notes on exceptions.
- GST coding corrections.
- Client queries and responses.

## Output

The bank account should be fully reconciled, with any exceptions documented before GST or year-end work proceeds.
